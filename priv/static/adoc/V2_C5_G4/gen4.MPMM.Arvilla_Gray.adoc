= Arvilla Gray (1808 - 1888)= Arvilla Gray Fisher

Born on January 4, 1808 in Paris, Maine. Died on July 14, 1888 in Dedham, Massachusetts. Married to Albert Fisher on August 31, 1834. Daughter of link:https://github.com/sparrell/CfsJksAs/blob/main/priv/static/adoc/V2_C5_G5/gen5.MPMMP.John_Gray.adoc[John Gray] and link:https://github.com/sparrell/CfsJksAs/blob/main/priv/static/adoc/V2_C5_G5/gen5.MPMMM.Rhoda_Andrews.adoc[Rhoda Andrews]. Wife to Albert Fisher. Mother to link:https://github.com/sparrell/CfsJksAs/blob/main/priv/static/adoc/V2_C5_G3/gen3.MPM.Frances_A_Fisher.adoc[Frances Fisher] and listed as mother to link:https://github.com/sparrell/CfsJksAs/blob/main/priv/static/adoc/V2_C5_G2/gen2.MP.Albert_Amory_Fisk_Fisher.adoc[Albert AF Fisher].
