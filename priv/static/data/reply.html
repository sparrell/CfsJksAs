Facts and Events
Name Elizabeth Shaw Whaley
Gender Female
Birth[1] 6 Feb 1813 Montville, New London, Connecticut, United States
Marriage 22 Nov 1843 Uncasville, New London, Connecticut, United Statesto Elijah Genubeth Strong
Death[1] Aft 20 Jul 1870 Montville, New London, Connecticut, United States

The birth dates for Elizabeth and her husband suggest that she was five years older than Elijah. The 1860 Federal Census confirms this. He is listed as a factory worker.

The 1870 census lists her as a widow and homemaker. Her son Albert and her daughter Mary were living with her and working in a cotton mill.
